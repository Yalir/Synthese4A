GList* _JabberBuddy::resources

A sorted list of resources in priority descending order. This means that the first resource in the list is the "most available" (see resource_compare_cb in buddy.c for details). Don't play with this yourself, let jabber_buddy_track_resource and jabber_buddy_remove_resource do it.
